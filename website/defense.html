Why Self Defense is Important

Learning self-defense is crucial for women, especially in today's world where personal safety is a concern. It helps to build confidence, empowers women to protect themselves, and equips them with skills that can be used in dangerous situations.

Key Self Defense Techniques

1. Be Aware of Your Surroundings

Stay alert and aware of your environment. Avoid distractions like your phone in public areas.

2. Use Your Voice

Shout for help or to distract the attacker. It can give you time to react and deter the attacker.

3. Target Vulnerable Areas

Aim for sensitive areas like the eyes, nose, or groin if confronted by an attacker. Use your elbows and knees effectively.

4.Use Pepper Spray

An effective tool to disable an attacker temporarily. Make sure you understand how to use it properly.

5.Know How to Get Help

Have a plan for contacting authorities or seeking help if you find yourself in a dangerous situation
